# ReimburseMate — Demo Script (2-3 minutes)

## Opening (15 seconds)
"ReimburseMate is an Anna-native AI reimbursement agent. It turns messy receipts into structured reimbursement drafts with AI extraction, policy checks, and human approval."

## Part 1: Show the Landing (15 seconds)
- Point to the workflow steps: Upload → Extract → Policy → Review
- Click "Try Demo"

## Part 2: Demo Preset (30 seconds)
- Select the "☕ Café Team Lunch" preset
- "AI extracts the merchant, amount, date, tax, category, and even suggests a business purpose — all from raw receipt text."
- Show the extraction results card
- Point out confidence score and missing fields

## Part 3: Follow-up Questions (20 seconds)
- "When fields are missing, the app asks targeted questions — not a generic form."
- Show the follow-up screen with pre-filled AI suggestions
- Add a business purpose
- Click "Generate Reimbursement Draft"

## Part 4: Policy Checks (30 seconds)
- "The policy engine runs deterministic checks — no AI needed."
- Show the warnings panel
- Try the "⚠️ Above Threshold" demo to show the $150 approval requirement
- Show the error blocking approval

## Part 5: Human Review (30 seconds)
- "The human review screen is the core of this app."
- Show all extracted fields, warnings, and confidence score
- Click "Edit" to correct a field
- Show policy checks re-running after edit
- Click "Approve Draft"

## Part 6: Final Export (20 seconds)
- "The final screen shows approval status, checklist, and an exportable note."
- Show the generated reimbursement summary
- Click "Export Note" to download

## Closing (15 seconds)
"ReimburseMate fits Anna because it's a focused, stateful workflow with AI tools, deterministic rules, and mandatory human review. It's not a chatbot — it's a real productivity app."
